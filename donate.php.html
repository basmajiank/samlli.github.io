Donate to CASTLE

As a public/private partnership, we rely heavily on private donations to keep our unique program running and servicing the state of North Carolina. Tax-deductible donations can be made in one of two ways:

  • Donate online by visiting the Medical Foundation of North Carolina’s “Make Your Gift” page.
    Once on that page, choose “CASTLE/CCCDP 353957” from the “Select a Fund” list. Enter your donation amount and click the yellow “Add to Cart” button. Review and click the gray “Next” button at the bottom of the page. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your donation.
  • Mail a check payable to “The Medical Foundation of North Carolina” and mail to CCCDP/CASTLE at:
    • 5501 Fortunes Ridge Dr.
    • Suite A
    • Durham, NC 27713
    • Attn: Robert Humphreys

We appreciate your consideration of a donation to CASTLE!

To learn more about donating, visit the Medical Foundation of North Carolina’s Web site at www.medicalfoundationofnc.org

To learn more about CASTLE, visit our Web site at www.med.unc.edu/earandhearing/castle.
