Android WS2812B Development

intro

This year for my miniterm project I worked on developing and building an Arduino based controller for a WS2812B addressable RGB LED strip. This would allow me to use the LED strip to carry out a variety of tasks, such as displaying a rainbow wave, pulsing various colors, as a visualizer for music, and many more!

materials

The materials I used included:

  • Arduino Pro Micro
  • WS2812B Addressable LED Strip
  • MicroUSB FemaleBreakout Board
  • Two-way Switch
  • MicroUSB Male Plug
  • 10μF Electrolytic Capacitor
  • 560Ω Resistor
  • Lots of wire

process

Before I began this project, I had not had much experience using the Arduino board or the software, so I first started off by putting the name of the Pro Micro into almighty google. Google returned a list of several very helpful sources, including one by Adafruit covering the usage and pinout of the controller
