Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) applies to SupportCASTLE.org. This online privacy policy applies only to information collected through SupportCASTLE.org and not to information collected in person, by e-mail or by telephone. This Policy explains what information that we, CASTLE, collect about you and what we do with it. We reserve the right to modify this Policy at any time, and we will notify you of any material changes to the Policy by posting the new Policy on our Web site and changing the “Effective Date” posted at the bottom. We encourage you to check this page periodically for any changes.

By using SupportCASTLE.org, you acknowledge that you have read, understand and agree to be bound by this Policy or any modified Policy as posted. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use our Web site.

What information do we collect?

SupportCASTLE.org collects personal information that you provide to us online such as your:

  1. name;
  2. telephone number;
  3. city;
  4. state;
  5. ZIP code;
  6. and e-mail address.

We collect this information when you complete the online “Contact” form in order to submit a question or comment to SupportCASTLE.org, when you submit your information in order to bid on a Silent Aunction item, when you register for the Corn Hole for CASTLE tournament and/or when you register for the CASTLE Golf Tournament. You may also visit and use SupportCASTLE.org anonymously; although, you must submit some information to use the “Contact,” “Bid,” and/or “Register” features. Using the “Contact,” “Bid,” and/or “Register” features is/are optional. We also collect IP addresses and technical information (such as Web browser type and version, monitor resolution, operating system, etc.) when you visit and browse our Web site.

What do we do with the information we collect?

We use the information you provide to us through the “Contact” feature to reply to your inquiry. We use the information you provide to us through the “Bid” feature to record your item bid(s) for the Silent Auction. We use the information you provide to us through the “Register” feature to record team information for our Corn Hole for CASTLE and/or Golf tournaments. We may use IP addresses and technical information (such as Web browser type and version, monitor resolution, operating system, etc.) to:

  1. improve SupportCASTLE.org;
  2. analyze traffic to SupportCASTLE.org;
  3. and prevent abuse or misuse of the “Contact,” “Bid,” and/or “Register” features.

Information collected on SupportCASTLE.org is stored within the United States. We do not publicly display your information on our Web site.

Do you share the information you collect with third parties?

We do not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your personal information, including e-mail address, to third parties. In some cases, a third party service provider with which we have contracted may be granted limited access to your “Contact,” “Bid,” and/or “Register” information in order to manage certain aspects of the online services we provide, such as maintaining and troubleshooting our Web site.

We may share anonymous technical information we collect with third party service providers in order to improve our Web site and our visitors’ Web experiences, as well as to manage certain aspects of the online services we provide, such as maintaining and troubleshooting our Web site.

We may also disclose your “Contact,” “Bid,” and/or “Register” information in special cases if required to do so by law, court order or other governmental authority or when SupportCASTLE.org believes in good faith that disclosing this information is otherwise necessary or advisable, including, for instance, to identify, contact, or bring legal action against someone who may be causing injury to or interfering with the rights or property of SupportCASTLE.org and/or CASTLE, another user or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities.

Does SupportCASTLE.org use cookies?

No. We do not use cookies at this time. Cookies are small files that a Web site or its service provider transfers to your computer’s hard drive through your Web browser, if you allow it, which enables the site’s or service provider’s systems to recognize your Web browser and capture and remember certain information.

Is SupportCASTLE.org secure?

SupportCASTLE.org is committed to maintaining the security of your information and has measures in place to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. The “Bid” and “Register” features utilize Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt and protect your data.

Does this Policy apply to linked sites other than SupportCASTLE.org?

No. SupportCASTLE.org contains links to other Internet sites and resources, including the Medical Foundation of North Carolina’s Web site. By clicking on a link to a third party, you will be redirected from SupportCASTLE.org to a third party Web site. These third party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. SupportCASTLE.org therefore has no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. You should make sure that you read and understand the privacy policies of these sites and direct any concerns regarding external links to the site administrator of that third party Web site. SupportCASTLE.org does not collect or store your credit card information on SupportCASTLE.org servers.

Does SupportCASTLE.org comply with the regulations of COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act)?

Yes. We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 13 years of age and have taken steps to prevent inadvertent submission. Our Web site and online services are directed to visitors older than 13 years of age.

If I give you my e-mail address, do you send me e-mails?

SupportCASTLE.org may use your e-mail address to respond to your inquiries submitted from the “Contact” feature, to send a confirmation when you use the “Bid” or “Register” features and to follow up on “Bid” or “Register” submissions that have processing or fulfillment problems. If you use the “Bid” feature, you have the option to receive notice via e-mail (“Bid Notice”) when other visitors place bids on the item(s) for which you placed a bid(s). We will use the e-mail address you specify to deliver “Bid Notices.” At any time, you may request to be stop the “Bid Notices,” by using the “Contact” feature of our Web site or by contacting us by phone.

How can I contact SupportCASTLE.org about this Privacy Policy or my online records?

If you have any questions regarding the Policy or your online records, you may contact us by:

  1. calling us at (919) 419-1428;
  2. or contacting us online.

If you choose to mail us, be sure to include your full name, address, and telephone number.
